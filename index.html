Weekend Day Centre for Dementia Care

An informal social environment for people with dementia and their carers.

The Weekend Day Centre provide an informal weekend and bank holiday care facility for people with a formal diagnosis of dementia and their carers within the Stockport borough, including Marple.

We are open every weekend and bank holiday throughout the year (except Christmas Day). The Centre provides day care for up to 40 clients each day; carers are welcome to attend, or to have a break at home for the day.
